# Abilities & Inventory

Inventory:
- 5Ã—5 grid (25 slots), no stacking.
- Passive items apply bonuses simply by being in inventory.
- Some items grant active abilities while carried.

Ability slots:
- Player starts with 5 slots.
- Specific relics unlock +5 slots (total 10).
- Abilities are either spell-like (consume MP) or item-activated (consumable/reusable).
- All costs/effects are multiples of 5.
